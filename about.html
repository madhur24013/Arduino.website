Challenges & Solutions

Challenge 1: Obstacle Detection

Implementing reliable obstacle detection in various lighting conditions and with different types of obstacles.

Solution: Combined multiple ultrasonic sensors with advanced filtering algorithms to improve detection accuracy.

Challenge 2: Battery Life

Optimizing power consumption while maintaining cleaning efficiency.

Solution: Implemented smart power management system and efficient cleaning patterns.

Challenge 3: Smart Navigation

Developing efficient navigation patterns for optimal room coverage.

Solution: Implemented advanced path planning algorithms and room mapping techniques.
