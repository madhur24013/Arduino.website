Frequently Asked Questions

How does the obstacle detection system work?

The robot uses ultrasonic sensors to detect obstacles in its path. These sensors emit high-frequency sound waves and measure the time it takes for the waves to bounce back, allowing the robot to calculate distances and avoid collisions.

What is the battery life of the robot?

The robot is equipped with a 7.4V Li-ion battery pack that provides up to 4 hours of continuous operation on a single charge. The power management system ensures efficient battery usage.

How does the voice control feature work?

The voice control module uses advanced speech recognition technology to process voice commands. Users can give simple commands like "start cleaning," "stop," or "return to base" to control the robot.
